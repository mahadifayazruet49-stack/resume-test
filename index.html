About

SHEET 01 / NOTES

I'm Md Mahadi Fayaz, a Civil Engineering student at Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (RUET). I'm drawn to the discipline because it sits exactly at the intersection of calculation and consequence — every beam, slab, and foundation has to hold up under a load that doesn't care about good intentions.

My tagline, "Born to lose, live to win," says something about how I approach setbacks: not as verdicts, but as load tests. I'd rather fail early on something hard than play it safe on something small.

Outside coursework, I'm building the habits of an engineer who actually finishes things — site visits, structural sketches, and steady, unglamorous progress.
